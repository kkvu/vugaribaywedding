KELLY & ARTHUR

About

We're doing it. After nearly 8 years together, we're finally making things official. We're having a party and we want you to be there!

Thank you for being a part of our story, and we can't wait to celebrate with you.
Kelly and Arthur met when they were 18 years old on the first day of school at the University of Texas at Austin. They worked on class assignments and collaborated on projects together. School flies by, graduation passes, and they lose touch.

Worklife ensues and a few years later at the age of 24, they run into each other at good ol' Barbarella in Downtown Austin. Arthur didn't even know Kelly lived in Austin at the time. A week later, they go on their first date to 888 and have been inseparable ever since.

They have created incredible memories together - traveling the world, building their lives in Austin, and supporting each other as they follow their dreams. Through the ups and downs, they'll continue to take on life together....even when Arthur is being unbearingly annoying and Kelly don't have time for it. They are truly soulmates.
